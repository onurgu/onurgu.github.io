04 / About

A CV is a poor format
for a life.

I’m Onur, an Istanbul-based computer scientist who never learned to keep disciplines in their assigned rooms.

I lead data science work around understanding course content at Udemy and teach transformer-based pre-training at Boğaziçi University. My research lives in natural language processing—especially morphologically rich languages, explainability, and Turkish.

The rest of the time, I make art, write for humans, and follow ideas across borders that looked sensible only from far away.
